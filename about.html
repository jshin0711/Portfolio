About me

My entry into User Experience

One day as I listened to a podcast, one of the hosts mentioned her career in User Experience. I had never heard this term and my interest was piqued from the things she mentioned about her job. As soon as I began researching User Experience, I knew this was it.

Some of the elements of being a User Experience Designer that draws me the most include...

  • Using both creative and analytic skills
  • Working with and for people
  • Exploring new ideas, creative solutions
  • Always looking to make improvements

After attempting some self-study routes and self-led projects, I landed on CareerFoundry's User Experience Immersive Course. CareerFoundry's UX course helped build and practice my UX skillset through project-based learning (see Recharge).

Essential traits I'm bringing

  • Adaptable - I love change and newness. I’m flexible and quick to adapt to new situations.
  • Curious - I’ve been asking questions since I could talk and haven’t stopped.
  • Collaborative - As an extrovert, I love working and getting along with others.
  • Emotionally intelligent - I am intuitive with others’ emotions and pride myself in being able to respond calmly in stressful situations
